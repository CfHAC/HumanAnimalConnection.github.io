About Us

Centre for Human-Animal Connection (CHAC) is a team of humans and non-human animals
dedicated to improving the well-being of humans, one human at a time.

"Animals are such agreeable friends. They ask no questions. They pass no criticisms." - George Elliot

    Our mission

  • To enhance emotional well-being and foster personal growth through the human-animal bond.

Our values

  • Growth-Centered: We believe mental wellness is a lifelong journey. We nurture personal development, emotional learning, and inner strength in every program and interaction.
  • Ownership of Growth: To empower individuals to take charge of their mental wellness journey, cultivating resilience, self-awareness, and purpose.
  • Open Conversations: We create space for honest dialogue around mental health to reduce stigma and build trust in every interaction.
  • Do Good Together: We believe in collaboration, kindness, and collective action for a more sustainable, just, and caring world.
