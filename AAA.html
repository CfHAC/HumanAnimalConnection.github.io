About AAA

Animal Assisted Activities (AAA) offer a unique and effective approach to improve our clients' well-being by getting them involved in activities with animals. These animals may include dogs, cats, guinea pigs, and more. Each animal has its own unique way of helping individuals, ensuring a tailored experience based on their specific needs.

The presence of animals in therapy provides valuable non-human companionship and support. Extensive research has shown that animals' non-judgmental nature allows individuals to express their emotions and thoughts freely. Animal-assisted activities create a safe environment where participants feel comfortable opening up and engaging in introspection. These programs can be implemented in a variety of settings, including hospitals, schools, therapy centers, offices, and nursing homes.

Benefits of AAA

Reducing Stress and Anxiety: Interacting with trained animals has been shown to reduce stress and anxiety levels. Animals have a calming effect on individuals, promoting relaxation and overall well-being.

Improving Social Skills: Animal-assisted activities facilitate social interaction and improve social skills. Individuals have the opportunity to engage with animals and develop communication, empathy, and emotional regulation skills.

Enhancing Physical Health: Interacting with animals can have physical health benefits. Activities such as walking dogs or grooming horses provide opportunities for physical exercise, improving mobility and overall fitness.

Boosting Self-esteem: Animals provide unconditional acceptance and support, boosting self-esteem and self-confidence. Positive interactions with animals can enhance self-perception and foster a sense of accomplishment.

Providing Emotional Support: Animals offer emotional support and comfort to individuals. Their presence can help alleviate feelings of loneliness, depression, and anxiety, providing a source of companionship and emotional connection.

Improved Interpersonal Relationships: Engaging in animal-assisted activities can positively impact interpersonal relationships. By building trust and forming bonds with animals, individuals can develop and enhance their ability to form meaningful connections with others.

Informal AAA (School Visits)

Our Informal Animal-Assisted Activities bring CHAC to schools, hospitals, and community centers to provide a unique and heartwarming experience for participants of all ages and abilities. These visits offer a refreshing break from routine and a chance for individuals to experience the joy and healing power that animals bring into our lives.

During these visits, we bring in trained animals to offer joy, comfort, and companionship through casual interactions. With no structured format, these visits allow individuals to engage and bond with our animals in a relaxed and informal setting. Whether you are young, old, disabled or abled, a student, a patient, or a member of the community seeking some uplifting moments, our animals welcome all with open arms.

How it Works

In our Informal AAA Institutional Visits with Animals, we create an environment of relaxation and spontaneity. Our well-trained animals, accompanied by experienced handlers, engage in casual interactions with participants.

These visits have no structured format, allowing individuals to freely connect with our animals at their own pace. Participants can enjoy petting, cuddling, and spending quality time with the animals, promoting a sense of joy and emotional well-being.

Duration

The sessions span from 45 minutes to 90 minutes.

Age Group

Our informal animal-assisted activities cater to individuals of all age groups, from the young to the old.

We believe that the presence of animals has the power to transcend age barriers and provide a positive impact on everyone, irrespective of their age or life stage. Whether it's young children experiencing the wonder of animals or senior citizens finding comfort and companionship, our program is open to everyone.

Price

We understand the value of these heartwarming interactions, and we strive to make them accessible to as many institutions as possible.

For detailed pricing information and to discuss your specific needs, please reach out to us at hello@chacsg.com. We are dedicated to fostering meaningful connections and spreading happiness through our informal animal-assisted activities, and we look forward to making a difference in your community.

Contact

To schedule a memorable and heartwarming informal AAA Institutional Visit with Animals, please contact us at hello@chacsg.com. We look forward to spreading happiness and fostering human-animal connections together!

Play and Learn with Animals AAA:

Our "Play and Learn with Animals" AAA program offers a delightful experience that combines the magic of animal interactions with the power of play.

Designed to foster joy, connection, and well-being, these engaging sessions provide a safe and interactive environment for individuals to participate in play-based activities with our trained animals. Through playful interactions, participants can discover the profound impact of the human-animal bond on their emotional, social, and cognitive well-being.

Who is the Target Group?

"Play and Learn with Animals" is a welcoming and inclusive AAA program that caters to individuals of all ages and abilities.

Whether you're a curious preschooler exploring the wonders of the animal world or a teenager seeking a compassionate companion, our program caters to your unique needs and preferences. Our facilitators adapt activities and interactions based on the age and abilities of the participants, ensuring an enjoyable and enriching experience for everyone involved.

How It Works:

Our sessions are thoughtfully designed to promote engagement, joy, and well-being through a delightful blend of play-based activities and animal interactions.

Under the guidance of our experienced facilitators, participants will have the opportunity to form meaningful bonds with our animals, fostering a sense of empathy, trust, and comfort. The therapeutic benefits of the human-animal bond are enhanced through interactive games, exercises, and shared moments of play, creating a safe and supportive space for personal growth and connection.

Price:

At Centre for Human-Animal Connection, we believe in making animal-assisted interactions accessible to all. We offer flexible pricing options for our "Play and Learn with Animals" program, tailored to accommodate different budgets and settings.

For detailed information about our pricing structure and package options, please get in touch with us at hello@chacsg.com. We'll be more than happy to assist you in planning a memorable and transformative experience with our beloved animal friends.

Join Us:

Mindfulness Oriented Animal-Assisted Activities

Introducing MOA³ - Mindfulness Oriented Animal-Assisted Activities, a collaborative program by Centre for Mindfulness and Centre for Human-Animal Connection.

MOA³ is designed not only to improve the well-being of individuals but also to help cultivate a calm mind that fosters introspection. This transformative program is suitable for groups ranging from primary school to adults.

How it Works?

At MOA³, we recognize the significance of mindfulness and its harmonious integration with the human-animal connection. Our sessions offer a unique blend of mindfulness practices and animal-assisted activities, providing participants with a profound and enriching experience.

Guided by our certified mindfulness facilitators, participants will have the opportunity to explore mindfulness techniques while engaging with our trained animals. These interactions can lead to a deeper understanding of oneself and the world around them, ultimately contributing to personal growth and well-being.

Duration

One session lasts from 1.5 to 2 hours.

Target Group

We believe that everyone, from primary school children to adults, can benefit from this mindfulness-oriented approach. Our program is designed to accommodate diverse age groups and ensure that each participant gains meaningful insights from these sessions.

Contact

To learn more about MOA³ and its availability for your group, please reach out to us at hello@chacsg.com. Together, let's embark on a journey of mindfulness, self-discovery, and the transformative power of the human-animal connection.

Reflective AAA

Thoughtful Tails is a unique program designed by our organization to provide secondary school and youth participants with a transformative experience.
Combining the therapeutic benefits of animal interactions with didactic learning sessions, this program offers a holistic approach to personal development and growth.

How It Works

Through meaningful interactions with our trained animals and engaging didactic classes, participants will gain valuable insights and skills in areas such as conflict resolution and understanding one's love languages.

Thoughtful Tails aims to create an environment where participants can reflect on their experiences, cultivate emotional intelligence, and foster a deeper sense of empathy and self-awareness.

Duration

One session lasts from 1.5 to 2 hours.

Target Group

We recognize the importance of catering to the unique needs of young individuals, and our program is thoughtfully tailored to create a safe and supportive space for their personal growth.

By integrating the nurturing presence of animals with reflective learning, Thoughtful Tails seeks to empower our youth with essential life skills that will positively impact their well-being and future.

Contact

Join us on this journey of self-discovery and growth as we embark on an adventure with Thoughtful Tails.

To learn more about our program and its availability, please contact us at hello@chacsg.com. Together, let's create meaningful connections and build a brighter future for our youth through interactions with our beloved animal companions.
