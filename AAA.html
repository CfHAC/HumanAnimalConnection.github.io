About AAA

Animal Assisted Activities (AAA) offer a unique and effective approach to improve our clients' well-being by getting them involved in activities with animals. These animals may include dogs, cats, guinea pigs, and more. Each animal has its own unique way of helping individuals, ensuring a tailored experience based on their specific needs.

The presence of animals in therapy provides valuable non-human companionship and support. Extensive research has shown that animals' non-judgmental nature allows individuals to express their emotions and thoughts freely. Animal-assisted activities create a safe environment where participants feel comfortable opening up and engaging in introspection. These programs can be implemented in a variety of settings, including hospitals, schools, therapy centers, offices, and nursing homes.

Benefits of AAA

Reducing Stress and Anxiety: Interacting with trained animals has been shown to reduce stress and anxiety levels. Animals have a calming effect on individuals, promoting relaxation and overall well-being.

Improving Social Skills: Animal-assisted activities facilitate social interaction and improve social skills. Individuals have the opportunity to engage with animals and develop communication, empathy, and emotional regulation skills.

Enhancing Physical Health: Interacting with animals can have physical health benefits. Activities such as walking dogs or grooming horses provide opportunities for physical exercise, improving mobility and overall fitness.

Boosting Self-esteem: Animals provide unconditional acceptance and support, boosting self-esteem and self-confidence. Positive interactions with animals can enhance self-perception and foster a sense of accomplishment.

Providing Emotional Support: Animals offer emotional support and comfort to individuals. Their presence can help alleviate feelings of loneliness, depression, and anxiety, providing a source of companionship and emotional connection.

Improved Interpersonal Relationships: Engaging in animal-assisted activities can positively impact interpersonal relationships. By building trust and forming bonds with animals, individuals can develop and enhance their ability to form meaningful connections with others.

Informal AAA (School Visits)

Our informal AAA Institutional Visits with Animals offer a unique and engaging experience for various institutions, such as schools, hospitals, and community centers. These visits aim to provide joy, comfort, and companionship through casual interactions with trained animals. With no structured format, these visits allow participants to enjoy the presence of animals in a relaxed and informal setting, fostering positive emotions and connections.

Play! + AAA

Our Animal Assisted Activities (AAA) program with play aspects offers a unique and engaging experience that combines the therapeutic benefits of animal interactions with the power of play. Designed to promote joy, connection, and well-being, these sessions create a safe and interactive environment where individuals can engage in play-based activities with trained animals. Through playful interactions, participants can experience the positive impact of the human-animal bond on their emotional, social, and cognitive well-being.

Reflective AAA (Name TBC) - didactic + AAA

Animal Assisted Activities (AAA) with Didactic Classes is a unique program that combines the therapeutic benefits of animal interactions with didactic learning sessions. By integrating animal-assisted activities with educational components, this program offers a holistic approach to personal development and growth. Through meaningful interactions with trained animals and engaging didactic classes, participants gain valuable insights and skills in areas such as conflict resolution and understanding the five love languages.
